会议简介 (Program)

系综平均理论在高能理论物理中占有重要地位，尤其是在处理非平衡态系统和强相互作用粒子系统的研究中显示出其独特的价值。这些理论提供了一种理解量子混沌、黑洞信息悖论和全息对偶的新途径。 通过对大量粒子系统的统计分析，系综平均理论揭示了多体量子系统中的普遍性和统计行为，是近年来量子信息理论和量子计算领域研究的热点。高能理论中的系综平均会议将于2024年12月6日至8日在上海大学乐乎新楼举办。 此次会议是专注于集体平均理论的国际研讨会，目的在于汇聚全球的理论物理学家、数学家和量子信息理论家，共同探讨黑洞信息丢失问题、非费米液体行为、量子混沌和全息对偶性等前沿科学问题。 会议将讨论Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev (SYK) 模型及其推广在量子引力和黑洞物理中的应用，以及在量子计算和凝聚态研究中的创新应用。

本次会议将邀请多位国际著名学者进行主题演讲。会议预计将吸引60名参与者，其中包括30名来自中国的学者和30名国际学者。 本次聚焦研讨会的形式为2-3天的密集讨论，除了专题报告外，还将有自由讨论环节，以促进学者之间的交流与合作。此外，会议也将强调教学方面，确保来自不同背景的参与者能够理解相关研究的本质和未来方向。 通过此次国际会议，旨在推动和提升中国在高能理论中的系综平均等方向的研究，同时让更多的国际同行了解国内相关研究，并推动国际合作与交流。我们鼓励国内外学者积极参与，共同探索这一激动人心的科学领域。

The ensemble averaging theories hold a significant position in high-energy theoretical physics, particularly in the study of non-equilibrium systems and strongly interacting particle systems, where they demonstrate their unique value. These theories provide a new approach to understanding quantum chaos, the black hole information paradox, and holographic duality. By statistically analyzing systems with a large number of particles, ensemble averaging theory reveals universality and statistical behavior in many-body quantum systems, making it a hot topic in recent years in the fields of quantum information theory and quantum computing. The workshop "Ensemble average theories in HET" is scheduled to be held at the LeHu New Building of Shanghai University from December 6th to 8th, 2024. This workshop is an international symposium focused on ensemble averaging theories, aiming to gather theoretical physicists, mathematicians, and quantum information theorists from around the world.

The workshop will invite several internationally renowned scholars to deliver their speeches. The workshop is expected to attract 60 participants, including 30 scholars from China and 30 international scholars. The format of this worshop will be an intensive discussion over the course of 2-3 days. In addition to talks, there will be free discussion sessions to facilitate communication and collaboration among scholars. Furthermore, the workshop will also emphasize the educational aspect, ensuring that participants from diverse backgrounds can comprehend the essence and future directions of the relevant research. Through this workshop, the aim is to promote and enhance research in China on ensemble averaging and other directions in high-energy theory, while also allowing more international peers to understand the relevant domestic research and to foster international collaboration and exchange. We encourage scholars from both domestic and international backgrounds to actively participate and jointly explore this exciting field of science.
